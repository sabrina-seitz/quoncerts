And their fantabulous Quoncerts

First they fell in love, then the magic happened: The married couple Ruth and Brooklyn Dekker started to make music under the name Rue Royale in 2006. Since then, the Anglo-American indie folk band has been touring both sides of the ocean all the way up and down. In 2020, when the world came to a halt due to the Coronavirus, they started playing so called Quoncerts to share their music with the world and their self-isolated audience.
